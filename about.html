Early Life

Christine was born in 1956, amidst Elvis Presley's rock-and-roll glory days. Christine's father was a mathematician and school teacher. As she recalls, "having your father as a high-school teacher is a bit embarrassing."

Early to Late Adulthood

Christine worked part-time at a grocery store to pay her way through college, obtaining a Bachelor's of Accounting in 1978 and, soon after, CPA accreditation. Christine is a retired IRS agent who spent much of her career auditing insurance corporations.

Retirement

Christine has a daughter who works for the American healthcare software company, EPIC Systems. In her time away from her daughter, she volunteers, participates in a dragon boat racing club, and cares for her cats.

Christine

Christine stands as a testament to boundless compassion, pouring her heart into family, into strangers from every walk of life, and even into the gentle souls who purr, creating a legacy of love that will echo far beyond her presence.
